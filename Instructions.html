Home Cards for Display Game Strategy

Instructions

Follow the instructions below to get started with the game:

1. The game takes place over one round. To start a round, the player/s will choose the Home Card and keep the title face-down.

2. Based on the riddle or question choose the next Action Card (10 cards) and carry on until all cards have been solved/performed.

3. Some Action cards have an associated Hint Card.

4. The title of action card and picture of hint card must be correlated.

5. NOTE: It is helpful to group cards in the sequence as they are performed.

Make sure to read the card instructions carefully and strategize to gain an advantage.
