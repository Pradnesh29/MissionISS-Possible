Game Strategy

To become a master at the game, here are some strategies you should consider:

  • Focus on Early Points: Gain as many points as possible in the early stages of the game.
  • Manage Your Resources: Keep track of resources and use them wisely to outsmart your opponents.
  • Adapt to Your Opponent: Observe your opponents' strategies and adapt your game plan accordingly.
  • Balanced Approach: Don't focus on just one strategy; balance between offensive and defensive moves.
  • Timing is Key: Make sure to play key cards at the right moment to maximize their effectiveness.
