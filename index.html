Welcome to Mission I.S.S. - Possible

Have you ever wanted to feel the thrill of space and enjoy the mysteries aboard the International Space Station (ISS)? Well, look no further than “Mission I.S.S.-possible.” Mission I.S.S.-possible is an intergalactic escape room card game, following the Phoenix 5 mission crew and their daring attempts to flee the ISS as it hurls towards Earth. This game is designed to challenge the minds and bodies of fellow astronauts when subjected to the harsh environment of microgravity and long-duration spaceflight.
