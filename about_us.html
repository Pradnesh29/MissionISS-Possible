About Us

Welcome to our game! Our team of passionate game developers, designers, and strategists have come together to create a unique and immersive experience for players around the world. We believe in crafting games that challenge the mind, inspire creativity, and foster a sense of community among players.

We started this journey with a shared love for gaming and innovation. With backgrounds in technology, design, and storytelling, we combine our skills to create engaging gameplay that is fun and thought-provoking.

Meet the Team

Manisha Prakash

Lead Game Designer

Manisha Prakash is an aerospace professional with a master's degree in Aerospace and Mechanical Engineering from the Illinois Institute of Technology. She is currently an Engineering Specialist at Airbus, focusing on landing gears and brakes. Her passion for space exploration began at the age of six, and she draws inspiration from space agencies like ISRO and NASA. Raised in Bengaluru, India, in a family of engineers and doctors, Manisha’s journey has been supported by a strong STEM foundation and her family. She remains inspired by figures like Stephen Hawking and Kalpana Chawla and enjoys adventurous activities like skydiving.

Jenee' Fox

CAD Designer

Jenee' Fox, a Louisiana native, holds multiple degrees in Drafting and Design, Production Technology, and Engineering Technology. She spent 10 years in Piping and Mechanical Design before transitioning to a role in Power Generation. Currently, Jenee' serves as a Senior Planner and Schedule Coordinator for Entergy, working on a Combined Cycle Power Plant. She is also a Citizen Scientist, having discovered two provisional asteroids, and is a Solar System Ambassador for NASA/JPL. Recently accepted into the PoSSUM Scientist-Astronaut program, Jenee' is focused on advancing her career towards Space and Defense.

Pradnesh Mhatre

Website Developer

Pradnesh Mhatre, a space exploration enthusiast from Raigad District, holds a bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Pune and is pursuing a Master’s in Aerospace Engineering at Arizona State University. He has completed NASA's Proposal Writing and Evaluation Experience Academy (NWPEE) in Fall 2023 and graduated as a Scientist Astronaut for Project PoSSUM from the International Institute for Astronautical Sciences (IIAS). Since 2019, Pradnesh has published six research papers and actively contributes to the field, focusing on Human Spaceflight Safety. He co-founded an organization promoting STEM education among underprivileged children and has received the 'Raigad Bhushan' award for his efforts. Pradnesh has volunteered as a test pilot and social media manager at Space Nation, while his hands-on experience at the Gagarin Cosmonaut Training Center fuels his passion for space innovation.
